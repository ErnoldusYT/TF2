**Install:**

**Step 1:** Make sure TF2 is closed
**Step 2:** Put the cfg in:
Steam\steamapps\common\Team Fortress 2\tf\cfg
**Step 3:** Start TF2

**Default (Taunt Mode)**

Numpad 0 – Join taunt / default weapon taunt
Numpad 1 – Taunt slot 1
Numpad 2 – Taunt slot 2
Numpad 3 – Taunt slot 3
Numpad 4 – Taunt slot 4
Numpad 5 – Taunt slot 5
Numpad 6 – Taunt slot 6
Numpad 7 – Taunt slot 7
Numpad 8 – Taunt slot 8

**While holding Numpad + (Enemy Disguises)**

Numpad 0 – Drop disguise
Numpad 1 – Enemy Scout
Numpad 2 – Enemy Soldier
Numpad 3 – Enemy Pyro
Numpad 4 – Enemy Demoman
Numpad 5 – Enemy Heavy
Numpad 6 – Enemy Engineer
Numpad 7 – Enemy Medic
Numpad 8 – Enemy Spy

**Release Numpad + to return to taunts.

While holding Numpad − (Friendly Disguises)**

Numpad 0 – Drop disguise
Numpad 1 – Friendly Scout
Numpad 2 – Friendly Soldier
Numpad 3 – Friendly Pyro
Numpad 4 – Friendly Demoman
Numpad 5 – Friendly Heavy
Numpad 6 – Friendly Engineer
Numpad 7 – Friendly Medic
Numpad 8 – Friendly Spy
